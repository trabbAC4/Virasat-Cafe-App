About Us Page

Our mission is to deliver quality sweets, treats and delights to the South Asian community in Jackson Heights as well as introducing our wonderful cuisine to others around New York City. We hope to connect others who don't share the same cuisine as us as well as allow them to try South Asian cuisine.

In addition, we offer coffee, tea, cakes & a variety of sandwiches.
